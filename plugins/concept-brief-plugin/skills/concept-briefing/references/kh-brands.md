# Hotel Brands

## The House Hotel (THHR)

**Positioning:** Boutique lifestyle hotel defining a new category of "Understated Contemporary Lux" — intimate, design-led, and rooted in the spirit of each location

**Tier:** Upper Upscale to Luxury

**Target guest:** Creative-industry professionals aged 30–45 — designers, architects, publishers, film producers; cosmopolitan design sophisticates who reject large chain hotels in favour of something personal and unique

**Design language:** Timeless, considered, and locally sensitive; buildings of architectural note with heritage and character; crafted interiors with fine detailing, local art, and a warm Mediterranean sensibility; never corporate, never generic

**Best fit for:** City-centre or culturally significant locations with strong local identity; buildings with heritage, architectural presence, or a story to tell; markets where discerning travellers seek boutique alternatives to international chains

**Guest personas:**

- **The Creative Professional** — Early 30s, works in fashion, design, or a related creative field. Single, driven, socially active. Wants a stylish base in a fashionable neighbourhood with a strong community feel. Brand-conscious and leads her social circle.

- **The Design-Savvy Couple** — Mid-30s, dual income, taste-driven rather than money-driven. City-breakers seeking a central location, great restaurant, spa access, and a genuine local experience. Laid back, adventurous, allergic to corporate hotels.

- **The Senior Creative on Assignment** — Mid-40s, film, media, or publishing. Extended stays while on location. Wants an interesting neighbourhood, gym, great food, and design that rewards attention. Confident, sociable, experience-led.

- **The Urban Resident** — Late 30s, professional, looking for a serviced apartment rather than a transient hotel stay. Wants to be in the right part of town, close to work, with concierge service and space to host. Culturally engaged, values experience over product.

## Cloud 7

**Positioning:** Local-loving, tech-forward, playful lifestyle hotel built for the next-generation traveller — genuine, stylish, and affordable

**Tier:** Midscale to Upper Midscale

**Target guest:** Curious, connected travellers of any age with a youthful mindset — fast-paced globe-trotters who want authentic experiences without paying luxury rates

**Design language:** Spontaneous, vibrant, and eclectic; colour palette shifts by environment (dark urban, terracotta for mountain, turquoise for coastal); open-plan social hubs replace formal reception; Cloud and "7" motifs adapted per property; sensory layering via music, scent, and dynamic lighting

**Best fit for:** Urban centres, secondary cities, and resort locations (coastal or mountain); new builds, conversions, or adaptive reuse; mixed-use developments where co-living, co-working, and hotel product can coexist

**Guest personas:**

- **The Connected Explorer** — Mid-20s to mid-30s, works remotely or in a creative/tech field. Travels frequently, values authenticity over luxury. Wants fast Wi-Fi, a social hub to work from, and local recommendations over generic hotel programming. Spends as much time in the lobby as in the room.

- **The Spontaneous Weekender** — Late 20s to early 40s, youthful mindset regardless of age. Short city breaks or quick getaways. Wants a stylish, affordable base that feels local — not a corporate chain. Drawn in by design, social energy, and a good F&B offer.

- **The Budget-Conscious Lifestyle Traveller** — Values design and experience but won't overpay for them. Midscale budget, upper-midscale expectations. Seeks genuine, well-considered spaces over bland standardisation. Cloud 7 is the brand that finally speaks to them in the midscale tier.
