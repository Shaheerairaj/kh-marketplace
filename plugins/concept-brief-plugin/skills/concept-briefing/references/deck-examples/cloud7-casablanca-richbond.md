CLOUD 7 RESIDENCE
AYLA AQABA
“Marrakech meets Mykonos by the Red Sea”.
Located within the Ayla Oasis, Cloud 7 forms part
of a large - scale, mixed - use urban development
in Aqaba, a vibrant coastal city in southern
Jordan along the Red Sea.
With 75 exquisite units, Cloud 7 offers a
welcoming, spacious, and modern environment,
quickly establishing itself as a leader in the local
hotel market since its opening in 2020.
In 2024, the Cloud 7 Residence achieved an
impressive RevPAR index of 193%, i.e. +93%
outperforming the competing hotels, including
Hyatt Regency, Movenpick, Kempinski,
InterContinental in Aqaba. This strong
performance has led to higher profitability for
the owners, which is the reason Kerten
Hospitality to open and operate a second hotel in
the Ayla Oasis development by 2026 for the same
owners.
With a vision to foster a community - driven,
qualitative living experience, Kerten Hospitality
plans to bring this success with you to Istanbul.
