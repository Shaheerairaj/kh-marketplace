01 VISION STATEMENT

THE VISION
Cloud7 Residence Jeddah
redefines extended stay living by
curating a lifestyle-driven
environment where leisure,
culture, and contemporary
design seamlessly converge.
Designed to reflect the rhythm
and richness of Jeddah’s
heritage, the property blends
local influences with modern
aesthetics and amenities,
creating a distinctive sense of
place. It offers guests an inspiring,
connected, and comfortable
experience. One that feels both
rooted and refreshingly forwardlooking.

THE MISSION
•Leverage local energy sources,
agriculture, and talent to create a
grounded and sustainable operation in
the heart of Jeddah.
•Integrate nature into our design
through biophilic principles, enhancing
guest wellbeing and the livability of
extended-stay units.
•Enliven the space with bold design,
curated art, and interactive cultural
moments that reflect Jeddah’s evolving
identity.
•Foster a connected community by
activating shared spaces with
meaningful programming — from social
events to knowledge-sharing and
wellness initiatives.
•Cater to today’s business guests by
offering efficient, design-led remote
work amenities and flexible communal
zones for collaboration.

02 MASTER PLANNING

THE RESIDENCE
THE STAY

The residential component will feature 120
thoughtfully designed, modern apartments
that are both spacious and light-filled. Each
unit reflects an elegant aesthetic, curated in
collaboration with local designers to blend
contemporary comfort with cultural relevance.
These apartments will be tailored to meet the
needs of consultants, modern families, and
groups of friends seeking a connected and
comfortable extended-stay in Jeddah.

02 MASTER PLANNING

THE RESIDENCE
THE STAY

The design blends a soft, earthy palette
of sand, ivory, clay, and muted blue —
evoking Jeddah’s coastal landscape
and desert tones. Interiors feature natural
oak finishes, woven rattan, brushed brass
details, and textured plaster walls for a
tactile, layered feel.
Subtle references to Hijazi patterns and
carved wooden accents pay homage
to traditional Jedawi architecture,
reinterpreted in a minimalist, modern
context. The result is a calm yet
character-rich environment that feels
distinctly local yet universally inviting.

WELLNESS COMPONENT
THE GYM
The gym at Cloud 7 residence Jeddah offers a
boutique fitness experience tailored for
extended-stay guests, with zones for cardio,
strength, and stretching. Designed with warm
tones and natural light, the space fosters
comfort and motivation. Guests can join
guided yoga and mat-based workouts, with
digital fitness sessions displayed on a large
screen for on-demand classes and routines.

COMMUNITY & CULTURE

THE IMPORTANCE OF
ART COLLABORATIONS
Cultural Authenticity bringing
authentic representation of the
region's culture and heritage into
the hotel environment.
Creating a distinctive and unique
identity to the hotel, setting it
apart from generic, standardized
accommodations and creating
a memorable experience for
guests.
Involving local artists fosters a
sense of community connection,
as it supports the local arts scene
and establishes a symbiotic
relationship between the hotel
and the community.

Local artists often incorporate
regional aesthetics, traditions,
and narratives into their work,
providing guests with a visual and
emotional connection to the
surroundings.
Hotels become cultural hubs by
showcasing local art, promoting
cultural tourism and encouraging
guests to explore and appreciate
the broader cultural context of
the destination.
