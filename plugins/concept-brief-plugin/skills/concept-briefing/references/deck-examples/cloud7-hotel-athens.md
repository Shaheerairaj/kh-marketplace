INTERIOR REFRESHMENTS
To ensure a smooth transition and immediate uplift of the asset, we propose a phased approach to the property’s refresh and enhancement.
The objective is to deliver quick, visible improvements from day one, while planning targeted upgrades over time, all without disrupting ongoing operations or
revenue performance.
In this context, we recommend working with Noor Studio, whose approach combines strong design sensibility with pragmatic, cost-conscious execution.
Their role is to define a light-touch PIP, focused on high-impact interventions rather than heavy repositioning. This phased structure allows for flexibility in
timing and investment, ensuring any capital deployed is measured, efficient, and value-accretive.
Phase 1 - Immediate
• Guestrooms carpet replacement (This will be applied across all room types, with potential variations depending on category and size.)
• Guestroom curtain replacement (This will be applied across all room types, with potential variations depending on category and size.)
• Signage improvements
Phase 2 – After 6 months
• Creation of a satellite kitchen on the rooftop
• Conversion of the back entrance of the building into the main F&B entrance
Phase 3 – After 12 months
• Transformation of the breakfast room into a social area that can still operate as a breakfast venue in the morning
(This involves a redesign of the space to function as a social area while maintaining breakfast operations in the morning)
• Enhancing of
• Guest room balconies and terraces landscaping
• Rooftop terrace

CLOUD 7 RESIDENCE
AYLA AQABA
“Marrakech meets Mykonos by the Red Sea”.
Located within the Ayla Oasis, Cloud 7 forms part
of a large - scale, mixed - use urban development
in Aqaba, a vibrant coastal city in southern
Jordan along the Red Sea.
With 75 exquisite units, Cloud 7 offers a
welcoming, spacious, and modern environment,
quickly establishing itself as a leader in the local
hotel market since its opening in 2020.
In 2024, the Cloud 7 Residence achieved an
impressive RevPAR index of 193%, i.e. +93%
outperforming the competing hotels, including
Hyatt Regency, Movenpick, Kempinski,
InterContinental in Aqaba. This strong
performance has led to higher profitability for
the owners, which is the reason Kerten
Hospitality to open and operate a second hotel in
the Ayla Oasis development by 2026 for the same
owners.
With a vision to foster a community - driven,
qualitative living experience, Kerten Hospitality
plans to bring this success with you to Athens.
