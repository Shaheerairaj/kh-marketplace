ALULA

Dar Tantora The House Hotel showcases AlUla's rich artistic heritage through
its traditional murals, which adorn the hotel walls and the wider Old Town.
These murals, historically painted as wedding gifts, depict local life, flora,
fauna, and symbols of happiness.
Architect Shahira Fahmy's team meticulously restored these artworks, including
those on wooden doors, in collaboration with archaeological experts and
local craftsmen. The Al-Dirah Art School played a crucial role by researching
and recreating AlUla's traditional color palette using pigments sourced from
local rocks and plants, ensuring historical accuracy in the restoration.

•
