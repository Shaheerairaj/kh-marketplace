and residents alike.

FINANCIAL PROJECTIONS

CONSIDERATION & ASSUMPTIONS

• Projections are in EUR.
• Stabilisation is assumed by Year 3 of operations.
• F&B revenue projections include the in-room dining service, as
well as from the Speakeasy bar.
• An upside to the F&B revenues (currently excluded) could come
from adapting a “dark kitchen” concept, which we can operate
simultaneously to the hotel operations as a dedicated delivery
kitchen for external orders (subject to further due diligence).
• We understand that the building includes 35 parking units. For
the purpose of the financial projections, we have assumed net
revenues of EUR 200 per parking space per month.
• The P&L assumes a qualitative high built asset that includes the
facilities and assumptions taken throughout this document.
• All assumptions are based on the most up-to-date
information available and are designed to deliver
the best and most efficient hotel concept to stand
out in the Warsaw hospitality market. They may be
adjusted following a site inspection and remain
subject to technical feasibility as assessed by the
owner’s architectural team.
