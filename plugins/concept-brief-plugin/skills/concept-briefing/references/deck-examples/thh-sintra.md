CONCEPT
METHODOLOGY
OUR APPROACH IS BUILT ON FIVE CORE PILLARS TO
ENSURE SUCCESSFUL DEVELOPMENT AND LONG-TERM
OPERATIONAL VALUE:

1

IDENTITY, SUSTAINABILITY & OPPORTUNITY
The concept celebrates Paris character, blending their cultural identity with a modern
lifestyle approach while staying aligned with development guidelines and the area’s
potential.

2

TARGET MARKETS & GUEST APPEAL
We target domestic and international leisure guests, leveraging the project
accessibility and area program

3

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
In-depth market analysis reveals gaps and opportunities, enabling us to position the product
competitively both locally and internationally.

4

VALUE-COST BALANCE
We strive for a balanced (I) cost/investment, (II) value creation, and (III) long-term
returns to maximize the property's future valuation.

5

KERTEN HOSPITALITY STRENGTHS
Leveraging from our network and local expertise of creating destinations with
innovative lifestyle driven brands.

CLOUD 7
RESIDENCE AYLA
AQABA
“Marrakech meets Mykonos by the Red Sea”. Located
within the Ayla Oasis, Cloud 7 forms part of a largescale, mixed-use urban development in Aqaba, a
vibrant coastal city in southern Jordan along the Red
Sea.
With 75 exquisite units, Cloud 7 offers a welcoming,
spacious, and modern environment, quickly
establishing itself as a leader in the local hotel market
since its opening in 2020.
In 2024, the Cloud 7 Residence achieved an
impressive RevPAR index of 193%, i.e. +93%
outperforming the competing hotels, including Hyatt
Regency, Movenpick, Kempinski, InterContinental in
Aqaba. This strong performance has led to higher
profitability for the owners, which is the reason Kerten
Hospitality to open and operate a second hotel in the
Ayla Oasis development by 2026 for the same
owners.
With a vision to foster a community-driven, qualitative
living experience, Kerten Hospitality plans to bring this
success with you to Portugal.

CLOUD 7
RESIDENCE ALULA
Creating a destination from the ground up, we
transformed a temporary 150-unit film camp into
Cloud 7 Residence AlUla, a vibrant mixed-use
community – now the largest hotel establishment in the
region with more than 300 keys.
Cloud 7 Residence AlUla today offers extensive
facilities including event spaces, outdoor pool, padel
courts, fitness areas, dining, and leisure concepts –
positioning it at the heart of AlUla’s social and cultural
life.
The property quickly became the best MPI performer in
AlUla, demonstrating strong market traction despite its
scale. Its success is a testament to our ability to turn
even unconventional starting points into destinationdefining projects.
This achievement led directly to our commission for Dar
Tantora The House Hotel, a 30-room luxury property
developed on centuries-old mudbrick fundaments.
