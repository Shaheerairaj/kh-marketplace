VISION STATEMENT

THE VISION
Cloud 7 Hotel & Residence Jumeirah will transform the
existing hotel into Dubai’s first lifestyle-casual, art-driven
beach hotel and residence. A destination merging
Venice Beach energy with Kite Beach culture.
Youthful, social, creative and spontaneous.
The transformation will embrace what the site already
offers, generous unit sizes, townhouses, villas, greenery,
without heavy structural interventions. Instead, we aim to
reposition through design, lifestyle programming,
partnerships, and smart amenity upgrades.

PROPERTY IMPROVEMENT PLAN
The following slides outline our recommended PIP considerations, shaped by our creative direction and the vision for
Cloud 7 Jumeirah’s transformation into an art-driven, lifestyle-forward beach destination.
PUBLIC SPACES
Our proposed upgrades include enhancements to the lobby, main restaurant, and gym, as well as the repurposing of the
3-bedroom unit in line with the overall design narrative and guest experience we are curating.

GUEST ROOMS – PHASE 1 (FACELIFT)
We propose an initial uplift for all guest rooms through new FF&E and vinyl wrapping of kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, and
internal doors. This approach offers a cost-effective way to refresh the interiors and bring them in line with the elevated
aesthetic of Cloud 7.
GUEST ROOMS – PHASE 2 (FULL RENOVATION)
A phased full renovation is recommended to align with the design direction. This would include new tiling, modernized
ceiling treatments, updated sanitary fixtures, new cabinets and wardrobes, refreshed terraces, improved villa access
points, and upgraded glazing elements.
BOH & PLANT ROOMS
We will require a full assessment of back-of-house and plant rooms once accessible to incorporate into our PIP.

THE HOTEL
Cloud 7 Hotel, designed for short-stay guests seeking
beachside energy, creativity, and convenience, will

personal, memorable, and connected to the lifestyle of
Dubai’s Kite Beach.

WELLNESS SPACE
THE POOL

The pool at Cloud 7 Jumeirah brings a sun-drenched
Venice Beach atmosphere to the property, offering two
moods depending on the day. Weekdays stay relaxed and
easygoing for guests who want quiet lounging, while
weekends come alive with upbeat music, social moments,
and darty-style day activations adding a playful LA edge.

The adjacent pool bar serves beach-inspired beverages
and light, sun-friendly dishes, creating an effortless lunch
and drinks experience. Together, the pool and pool bar
form a lively social setting where guests can choose
between calm weekday downtime or energetic weekend
gatherings that reflect the expressive spirit of Cloud 7.

WELLNESS SPACE
THE SPA

The spa will channel retro Venice Beach beauty and wellness,
offering a fresh, energetic take on recovery and self-care.
Rather than a traditional spa environment, the focus is on
modern, results-driven treatments inspired by beach culture,
movement, and contemporary beauty rituals.

Guests can access express cryotherapy, physio-led recovery
sessions, IV vitamin drips, targeted skin treatments, and hair
rituals prioritizing non-heat techniques, including heatless curl
methods using soft rollers for effortless, beach-inspired texture.
Seasonal collaborations with specialist studios and practitioners
are envisaged to sustain interest and curiosity of members and
the local community.

CASE STUDY & COLLABORATIONS

CLOUD 7 AYLA AQABA RESIDENCE – A CASE SCENARIO FOR CLOUD 7 JUMEIRAH
The Cloud 7 Ayla Aqaba Residence is located at Ayla Oasis, a large mixed-use development in Aqaba, a
vibrant Red Sea destination in Jordan. With 75 exquisite units, Cloud 7 offers a welcoming, spacious, and
modern environment, quickly establishing itself as a leader in the local hotel market since its opening in 2020.
The creative design brief “Mykonos meets Marrakech on the Red Sea” was perfectly executed in conjunction
with the Owner and has been a resounding success.
In 2024, the property achieved a RevPAR index of 193%, outperforming competing hotels by +93%, including
Hyatt Regency, Movenpick, Kempinski, InterContinental in Aqaba. This strong performance has led the Owner
to award Kerten Hospitality the operation of a second hotel in 2026.
With a vision to foster a community-driven, qualitative living experience, Kerten Hospitality plans to bring this
success with you to Dubai
