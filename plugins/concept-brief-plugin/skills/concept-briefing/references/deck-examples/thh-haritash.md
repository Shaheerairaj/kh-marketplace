A MIXED-USE, ESG AND LIFESTYLE OPERATOR

Kerten Hospitality is excited to present a strategic vision for HaritasH Hospitality’s entry into the
hospitality sector, encompassing a diverse collection of assets across key destinations in
India. Drawing on Kerten’s expertise in creating design-led, community-driven, and
experience-focused lifestyle brands, this proposal aims to position each asset under a clearly
defined identity while unlocking operational efficiency and long-term value across the
portfolio.
With a proven track record across Europe, the Middle East, and emerging markets, Kerten
Hospitality brings a holistic, brand-led operational approach tailored to each location. From
spiritual and nature-driven destinations to high-growth urban, each property will be
thoughtfully aligned within Kerten’s ecosystem of brands, including Hosme, Cloud 7, and The
House. This approach allows every asset to express its unique character while benefiting from
shared standards, systems, and a cohesive guest journey rooted in authenticity, local
connection, and contemporary lifestyle positioning.

ALULA

Dar Tantora The House Hotel showcases AlUla's rich
artistic heritage through its traditional murals, which
adorn the hotel walls and the wider Old Town. These
murals, historically painted as wedding gifts, depict
local life, flora, fauna, and symbols of happiness.
Architect Shahira Fahmy's team meticulously restored
these artworks, including those on wooden doors, in
collaboration with archaeological experts and local
craftsmen. The Al-Dirah Art School played a crucial role
by researching and recreating AlUla's traditional color
palette using pigments sourced from local rocks and
plants, ensuring historical accuracy in the restoration.
