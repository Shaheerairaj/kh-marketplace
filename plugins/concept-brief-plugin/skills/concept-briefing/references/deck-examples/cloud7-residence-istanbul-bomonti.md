CONCEPT
FOREWORD
Cloud 7 Residence Istanbul Bomonti aspires to redefine hospitality and urban living in
Istanbul - a bold fusion of branded apartments, lifestyle hospitality, community spaces, and
curated experiences in one of the city’s most dynamic neighborhoods.

At Kerten Hospitality, this is our forte. We specialize in creating and operating lifestyledriven, mixed-use projects that blur the boundaries between hospitality and living, curating
and coordinating a wider ecosystem (non-exhaustive references can be found as of page 18).
Our suggestions focus on maximising value through:
• Serviced apartments for short- to mid-term stays with hotel-style operations.
• Branded apartments for rent with services, security, and community activation, supporting
higher rents and creating a lively atmosphere.
• Shared amenities tailored to the location and scale of the building, ensuring strong usage
(between hotel guests and residents), higher perceived value, and additional revenues.
This setup enhances the resident and guest experience, strengthens the asset’s diversity and
income, and ensures lean operations across all elements to maximise proceeds.
These recommendations are based on the limited information available, and we look forward
to refining them together as we progress with the project.

Main Restaurant & The Living Room
Around 50 seats plus a multifunctional space for private events and community activation.
A vibrant feature for both residents and hotel guests, adding variety and appeal to the
overall concept. (Alternatively, this could be located above the technical floor)

Branded Apartments for Rent – Flexible

living space. Supported by à la carte hotel services, creating two distinct apartment
offerings in the building (see the following point)
Branded Apartments for Rent – Furnished
Cloud7 Residence-branded apartments, fully furnished to provide a turnkey living
solution. These benefit from economies of scale through shared FF&E procurement with
the hotel, offering higher rental value and cost efficiency. Includes à la carte hotel

Technical Floor

Serviced Apartments (Hotel-Style)
Designed for short- to mid-term stays, with the hotel as the central driver of services and
operations. Managed by Kerten Hospitality to maximise perceived value, create synergies
across the building, and ensure lean, professional operations.

Reception, Wellness & Fitness (+ Retail)
Includes a reception integrated with a café and grab-and-go station, supplied directly by

community (should be aligned with the overall concept)

Located within the Ayla Oasis, Cloud 7 forms part

outperforming the competing hotels, including
Hyatt Regency, Movenpick, Kempinski,
InterContinental in Aqaba. This strong
performance has led to higher profitability for
the owners, which is the reason Kerten
Hospitality to open and operate a second hotel in
the Ayla Oasis development by 2026 for the same
owners.
With a vision to foster a community-driven,
