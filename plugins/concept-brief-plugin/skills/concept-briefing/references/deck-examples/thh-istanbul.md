CURATED STAYS

ACCOMMODATION
The House Residence Istanbul Bahçelievler offers 103 residences - split into two,
three, and four-bedroom units - each carrying the unmistakable The House
aesthetic - warm materiality, layered textures, and interiors that feel collected
rather than decorated. Fully serviced living amenities mean every detail of daily
life is effortlessly taken care of, leaving residents free to simply live, beautifully.

SPA & HEALTH CLUB
Rooted in the rhythms of Istanbul and the timeless traditions of the
hammam, The House Hotel & Residence wellness experience
transcends the ordinary spa - a sanctuary of steam rooms, treatment
rooms, and a longevity clinic where ancient ritual meets modern science.

“Marrakech meets Mykonos by the Red Sea”. Located

mixed-use urban development in Aqaba, a vibrant coastal

With 75 exquisite units, Cloud 7 offers a welcoming,

in 2020.

RevPAR index of 193%, i.e. +93% outperforming the

Kempinski, InterContinental in Aqaba. This strong

which is the reason Kerten Hospitality to open and operate
a second hotel in the Ayla Oasis development by 2026 for
the same owners.
With a vision to foster a community-driven, qualitative living

COMMERCIAL TERMS
PROPERTY MANAGEMENT
11.

PROPERTY MANAGEMENT

The property (& community) management of the entire Project shall be managed by the Kerten Hospitality, including all common areas, services, facilities, facilities management,
and other offerings.

12.

PROPERTY MANAGEMENT FEES

Ten percent (10%) of the service charge paid by owners of the Branded Residences in accordance with the annual service charge budget. The service charge shall mean all
costs incurred in connection with the property management of the building in line with international first-class residences, including but not limited to general property
maintenance, cleaning of public areas, gardening, leisure facilities, security, and administration functions.

13.

À-LA-CARTE SERVICE FEE

A ten percent (10%) fee shall be applied to the cost (whether direct of indirect) of providing services utilized directly by individual residents or other users of the building,
including but not limited to housekeeping, laundry, delivery or pick-up services, spa or wellness treatments, and personal training. Such services, being discretionary in nature,
shall be accounted for under a separate profit and loss statement.

14. OTHER ASSUMPTIONS

Scope of Kerten Hospitality (KH) – KH will provide operational and administrational services for the branded apartments, that may include the hotel´s resources, e.g. human
resources, to perform the services cost efficient and prudently, such as:
•

Reception and concierge services

•

Wellness and fitness operations

•

Community activation and programming

•

Maintenance and cleaning of common areas

•

Security for residential common areas

•

A la carte services (e.g., housekeeping & laundry, babysitting, etc.)

•

Other services, as may be agreed during the development and operational planning process

KH shall act as the primary point of contact for residents regarding all matters related to the building management and services within Kerten Hospitality’s remit. Other matters
shall be directed to the Developer-appointed asset manager who bears the responsibility of timely collection of the service charge to ensure appropriate quality is maintained
on property throughout the term of this agreement.
