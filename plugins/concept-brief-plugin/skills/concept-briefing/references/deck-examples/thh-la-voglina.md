CURATED STAYS

ACCOMMODATIONS
The House Hotel La Voglina features 50–60 exquisitely appointed suites
across two buildings. The historic villa retains its original architectural
charm, including frescoed ceilings, thoughtfully refurbished to blend
timeless elegance with modern comfort. The adjoining new buildings
offer contemporary suites with a refined design, seamlessly
complementing the heritage of the estate while providing all the
amenities of a luxury lifestyle destination. The hotel is dog-friendly, with
optional dog-sitting services, ensuring every member of the family
enjoys a memorable stay.

CULINARY JOURNEYS

BREAKFAST HALL
Guests begin their day in the majestic breakfast hall, a grand central
room in the heart of the historic villa. Rich in architectural detail and
timeless elegance, this stunning space offers both à la carte and buffet
options, setting the tone for a culinary journey that blends heritage,
sophistication, and thoughtfully curated flavors. By day, it transforms
into a relaxed lounge for socializing and unwinding, and by evening, it
becomes an elegant venue for exclusive events and gatherings.

| 12

CULINARY JOURNEYS

OSTERIA
In the left wing of the historic villa, our farm-to-table Osteria brings the
flavors of Piedmont to life, with every dish crafted from ingredients
grown in our own gardens. A destination for both guests and locals,
the Osteria promises an immersive dining experience where
seasonal, sustainable cuisine meets the warmth of an inviting,
elegant setting. A place to savor, celebrate, and linger.

| 13

CULINARY JOURNEYS

FINE DINING RESTAURANT
Our flagship fine dining restaurant brings a world-class culinary
experience to the heart of the historic villa. Guided by an iconic
chef, the restaurant will offer a contemporary interpretation of
Piedmontes cuisine, blending local heritage with innovative
techniques. The setting is designed to be as unforgettable as the
cuisine itself: an elegant, intimate space that celebrates history,
craftsmanship, and the art of dining.

SOCIAL SALONS

LOUNGE BAR
The House Hotel La Voglina offers curated spaces designed for
connection, conversation, and celebration. Our elegant cigar
lounge features a carefully curated selection of best local and
international wines from our wine cellar, with regular wine-tasting
evenings and curated events that invite guests and locals alike
to gather, socialize, and savor the finest flavors in a
sophisticated, welcoming setting.

| 15

SOCIAL SALONS

COLLABORATION HUB
Located in the Casa dei Coloui building with striking double-height
ceilings, our versatile meeting and conference spaces are designed
for both productivity and elegance. Equipped for business retreats,
workshops, and corporate events, these rooms can also transform into
ballrooms or pop-up venues, offering a unique blend of functionality
and sophistication. Whether hosting a professional gathering or an
exclusive celebration, the Collaboration Hub provides an inspiring
setting that seamlessly combines work, leisure, and lifestyle.

WELLNESS ESCAPES

SPA & FITNESS
The right wing of the historic villa is devoted to wellness and active
living, featuring a luxurious spa with independent access for
ultimate privacy. Guests can indulge in steam rooms, indoor and
outdoor saunas with cold plunges, and tailored treatments
designed to rejuvenate body and mind. The wellness experience
is complemented by a gym, an outdoor heated infinity pool,
outdoor training machines, and tennis and paddle courts, offering
a seamless combination of relaxation, fitness, and invigorating
open-air activity in a serene, elegant setting.

| 19

GARDENS

GARDENS & OUTDOOR ACTIVITIES
Spanning 20 hectares, the estate of The House Hotel La Voglina is a sanctuary of
nature, leisure, and discovery. Guests can explore our productive gardens and
farm, where fresh fruits, vegetables, and herbs are grown to enrich our culinary
experiences, while a variety of animals, such as chickens, ponies, horses, and
alpacas, bring the estate to life. Wander through the romantic rose garden or
gather around cozy fireplaces and elegant lounges for moments of relaxation
and connection. Every corner of the estate is designed to inspire, delight, and
immerse guests in the rhythms of the land, creating unforgettable experiences in
a setting of timeless beauty and refined elegance.

EXPERIENCES

COMMUNITY ACTIVATION
At the heart of The House Hotel La Voglina lies a deep connection
to its surroundings and people. To bring this vision to life, we are
introducing Riccardo, our local brand ambassador, a renowned
jeweler born and raised in the village. With his roots deeply
intertwined with the community, Riccardo will act as the bridge
between the hotel and the local culture: curating encounters,
storytelling, and exclusive access to hidden treasures of the region.
Through his guidance, guests will not only experience a place to
stay, but a vibrant community to discover, making The House Hotel
La Voglina a true cultural landmark.

| 24

GASTRONOMY
At The House Hotel La Voglina, gastronomy becomes an
adventure. Guests are invited to uncork the secrets of Piedmont
through private wine tastings with rare vintages, savor the thrill of
truffle hunting followed by indulgent tastings, and step into the
kitchen for chef-led masterclasses that transform local ingredients
into culinary art. Rooted in our very own estate gardens and farm,
these experiences are not only about flavor, they are about
discovery, connection, and the joy of creating unforgettable
memories around the table.

| 25

RETREATS
A retreat at La Voglina is more than a getaway, it is a purposeful
escape. Whether It is wellness seekers recharging in our spa,
businesses reconnecting through strategy and team-building, or
creatives finding inspiration in Piedmont’s beauty, La Voglina
provides the space, resources, and atmosphere for transformation.
Surrounded by history, nature, and community, every retreat
becomes an unforgettable journey that restores clarity, sparks
creativity, and strengthens connection

| 26

OUTDOOR ACTIVITIES
Beyond its natural splendor, La Voglina offers a playground for
movement and exploration. Guests can ride through the estate on
horseback, cycle along scenic trails, or test their skills on private
tennis and padel courts. Sunrise yoga in the gardens, open-air
fitness circuits, and nearby world-class golf clubs invite both
relaxation and challenge. Curated picnics beneath ancient trees,
outdoor training, and seasonal activities ensure every day is rich
with energy, discovery, and unforgettable moments in the open air.

| 27

JEWELRY JOURNEYS
Piedmont has long been a cradle of Italian jewelry craftsmanship,
and The House Hotel La Voglina opens the doors to this dazzling
world. Guests are invited on private tours of renowned jewelry
ateliers and factories, witnessing master artisans at work and
uncovering the secrets of timeless Italian design. Intimate workshops
offer hands-on opportunities to create bespoke pieces, while
exclusive showcases and trunk shows bring rare jewels directly to
the pop-up space in building B1. More than a glimpse into luxury,
these experiences celebrate heritage, artistry, and the enduring
brilliance of Piedmont’s most precious tradition.
