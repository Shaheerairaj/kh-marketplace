Cloud 7 Hotel & Residences Arlberg will bring
a new kind of

inspired and welcomed.

natural materials, thoughtful design, and a strong sense of
place. Guided by Cloud 7’s approach to experience - led
hospitality, the space will host curated collaborations,
community - driven activations, and creative programming

SOCIAL LOBBY
The lobby lounge and bar is conceived as a
relaxed social heart of the hotel, a place to
pause, gather, and transition throughout the
day.
In the colder season, the lounge becomes a
welcoming retreat, serving hot chocolate,
spiced wine, and warming cocktails, paired
with light snacks and casual bites. Large
windows overlook snow - covered slopes and
passing skiers, creating a natural sense of
movement and atmosphere beyond the glass.

HOTEL ROOMS
The 137 hotel rooms and suites are designed
as warm, character - driven retreats that feel
relaxed, residential, and expressive. Natural
materials, tactile finishes, and confident colour
accents create spaces that invite guests to
slow down and settle in.
Each room is layered with personality through
curated lighting, custom details, and limited
edition artworks developed with local artists
and design schools, strengthening the
emotional connection to place.

SERVICED
APARTMENTS
Designed for longer stays, the serviced
apartments offer a calm, contemporary alpine
living experience. Each unit includes a
kitchenette, living area, and generous storage,
allowing guests to live comfortably and
independently.
Interiors draw from modern cabin living,
combining natural wood, warm textures, and a
restrained palette. Minimal accents keep the
spaces adaptable, familiar, and easy to make
one’s own.

APARTHOTEL
The aparthotel introduces a flexible living
model that blends residential comfort with
lifestyle hospitality. Intimate in scale, it
encourages a sense of community rather than
transient stays.

This hybrid format supports year - round use
and appeals to longer - stay guests seeking
autonomy without sacrificing service.

CHARCOOD
Charcood is Cloud 7’s fire - led dining concept,
located within the main hotel building, and
shaped around charcoal, craft, and seasonality.
An open grill anchors the space, turning
cooking into a social, sensorial experience that
connects guests through fire, flavour, and
atmosphere.
Menus evolve with the seasons, shifting from
rich, comforting dishes in winter to lighter,
produce - led plates in warmer months, while
remaining rooted in honest, elemental cooking

.

JOONTOS
Joontos is Cloud 7’s food - sharing concept,
located within the aparthotel building, and
inspired by the idea of coming together.
Derived from the Spanish word “Juntos,”
meaning together, the concept celebrates
communal dining and relaxed, social moments.
Encouraging guests to try and share the chef’s
best Mediterranean - inspired dishes, prepared
with the freshest ingredients sourced locally.
Each dish is given a modern twist while staying
true to local gastronomy.

A- LA - CARTE
SERVICES
The Cloud 7 Serviced apartments will offer a
curated menu of à la carte services extended
from the Hotel, designed to enhance everyday
living for its guests.
From access to the hotel's concierge
assistance and laundry services, the residents

THE SPA
Located below ground, the spa is conceived as
a calm, sheltered retreat away from the energy
of the slopes. Sauna facilities and a heated
pool anchor the experience.
Private treatment cabins, thermal elements,
and quiet relaxation zones support recovery,
rest, and slow moments after active days
outdoors.

especially for longer - stay guests.

PARTNERSHIPS
Partnerships with nearby farmers and
producers lie at the heart of Cloud 7’s
commitment to sustainability and community.
Our goal is to source majority of food and
beverage locally, with menus that evolve
naturally with the seasons.

Even the in - room experience reflects this
philosophy, with thoughtfully selected local
amenities and toiletries that minimize waste
and support regional makers, ensuring every
detail aligns with Cloud 7’s vision for a more
conscious and connected stay.
