VISION

MISSION

To create Riyadh’s most dynamic
lifestyle hospitality destination, a
vertical community where modern
travellers, weekend explorers, and
local residents converge to
experience a new kind of luxury.

To harness Riyadh’s energy by leveraging

build a grounded, future-forward
operation.

blends effortlessly with leisure,
where wellness is a philosophy, and
where bold design and Saudi
cultural expression come together
to tell a story that’s both local and
globally relevant.

hospitality in the Kingdom by
setting a new standard: one that is
casual yet elevated, social yet
personal, and built not just for
staying, but for living, connecting,
and thriving.

To infuse bold design, curated art, and
immersive cultural experiences that
reflect the capital’s evolving identity.
To foster meaningful connection by
activating shared spaces with
programming that inspires — from social
events and wellness rituals to knowledge
exchange.
To empower modern professionals with
flexible, design-led workspaces that
support collaboration, focus, and flow.

FOOD SOUK

Food Souk is a vibrant, all-day culinary destination
that reimagines the traditional food market
through a modern, curated lens. Inspired by
Mamlakah’s spirit of communal dining, it brings
together a rotating mix of chef-led stalls, concept
kitchens, and pop-up experiences creating a
social, ever-evolving hub for food lovers.
Designed for flexibility, Food Souk caters to every
rhythm of the day. Whether guests are grabbing a
quick bite between meetings, enjoying a casual
coffee catch-up, or settling in for a leisurely dinner.
With dine-in options, shared seating areas, and
convenient takeaway formats, it offers an
accessible, high-quality experience that fits
seamlessly into busy urban lifestyles.

2nd and 3rd Floors
Ouspace is a distinctive lifestyle, social, and business hub
designed to foster connection, knowledge-sharing, and
entrepreneurial growth. Set within a raw-yet-refined
environment shaped by clean lines and exposed materials it
caters to the evolving needs of the next generation of
professionals, while providing an undistracting and flexible
environment for work.
From private offices and hot desks to dedicated desks and
collaborative zones. This space allows for both productivity
and privacy. Beyond daily work needs, Ouspace is also
equipped to host corporate events, conferences, workshops
and banqueting functions, making it a versatile venue for
business gatherings and community engagement. Ousapce
is the ideal environment for work, networking, and highimpact events while maximizing revenue per square meter.

THE ROOMS
5th to 18th Floor
The hotel will offer 280 rooms that reflect a quiet,
confident simplicity. Spacious, light-filled, and
grounded in raw elegance. Brutalist elements like
exposed concrete and structured forms are
softened by warm textures, layered lighting, and
curated materials. Each room will feature
bespoke pieces by Saudi and GCC designers,
bringing in subtle cultural expression through
contemporary art and detail.
The space is thoughtfully designed to offer an
elevated yet understated stay in the heart of
Riyadh fit for consultants, modern families, and
groups of friends.

WELLNESS COMPONENT
THE GYM
20th Floor
Perched on the 20th floor, the wellness space at The House
Hotel and Residence Olaya is designed to be a shared
sanctuary for both hotel and residential guests.
Intentionally designed to encourage interaction, not
division, between short- and long-stay communities.
The gym combines functionality with contemporary edge;
concrete walls, industrial ceilings, and natural wooden
flooring set the tone for a strong and focused
environment. Zones are thoughtfully laid out for cardio,
strength training with soft lighting adding warmth to the
raw architecture.
A separate studio space allows for personal training, yoga,
and stretch sessions, whether solo or in small groups. It’s
wellness redefined. Stripped back, intentional, and
attuned to modern urban living.

THE IMPORTANCE OF
ART COLLABORATIONS
The art that will be showcased in
the Cultural Hub at Cloud7 Olaya
offers guests an inspiring pause a space to reflect, connect, and
discover. In-house guests can
explore the rotating exhibitions at
their leisure, while external visitors
can access the space through a
curated day-pass program.

The hub will feature works by
leading regional talents such as
Lulwah Al Homoud, known for her
intricate geometric abstractions;
Nasser Al Salem, whose
conceptual calligraphy explores
the intersection of language and
meaning; and Princess Reem AlFaisal, whose evocative blackand-white photography captures
the soul of Arab identity and
architecture. Together, these
exhibitions will transform the hub
into a living gallery. A refined,
ever-evolving reflection of the
region’s creative pulse.
