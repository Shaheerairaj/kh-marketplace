Positioning Thesis

The House Hotel's design-led "understated contemporary lux" positioning fills the unclaimed white space in Marrakech's Ennakhil corridor — between the commodity all-inclusive resorts of the Palmeraie and the ultra-luxury palaces of the medina/Hivernage.

Target — Design-savvy leisure, families, resort-MICE

03 / 06
Concept Direction

Four pillars.

01

Atlas Threshold

Every public space, restaurant, pool, and room oriented to the unobstructed Atlas silhouette and amber dust-light of the N9.

02

Living Maker Souk

A working craft village within the property — rotating Marrakech artisans producing limited pieces that flow into rooms, restaurants, and the boutique.

03

Garden as Story

The 5 ha plot as a single landscape composition — garden spine, kitchen garden, water rills referencing khettara heritage. Gardens as circulation.

04

Resort MICE Pavilion

A purpose-built 500–600 m² ballroom — the only lifestyle alternative to medina palaces for Pan-African and European corporate events.
